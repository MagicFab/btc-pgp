In order to use BTC/PGP, you must generate a bitcoin address, fund that address, and broadcast a transaction. Broadcasting a transaction will reveal your public key, giving other the ability to send you messages encrypted to your public key. Because this is a proof of concept, this app uses testnet, and address generation, funding, and transaction broadcast can all be done in the browser below.
